No account required. No cloud sync. Your campaign records stay on your machine unless you choose to export or share them.

Local-first storage Campaign data is saved locally through the desktop app.
Private by default No hosted generation calls or bundled user campaign data.
Original layout No official character sheet artwork, copied maps, or bundled adventure text.

Campaign tools without cloud lock-in

Campaign Dashboard

Track recent sessions, open hooks, party status, active quests, major NPCs, factions, and coming pressure.

Party Sheets

Use original Red Thread character sheets with D&D-style stats, skills, saves, inventory, features, spells, and PDF/JSON export.

Session Prep & DM Screen

Prepare scenes, clocks, hooks, likely NPCs, secrets, and table notes, then run from an expandable live screen.

Codex & Knowledge

Manage NPCs, factions, locations, quests, artifacts, secrets, timeline events, and player knowledge visibility.

Smart Local Links

Review suggested connections based on shared tags and direct name mentions before they become canon.

Backups & Restore Points

Export local JSON backups, validate imports, and keep restore points before destructive edits.
